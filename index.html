Skin health

Skincare routine Tips

Nowadays, there is a misconception that to have healthy looking skin you need to incorporate many products into your skin care routine. Architect Ludwig Mies van der Rohe once said "Less is more". A simple skin care routine and keeping a few aspects in mind will go a long way. Below are a few things to avoid for good skin:

Smoking

The toxins in cigarette smoke destroys the fibrous components like collagen and elastin which gives the skin strength and elasticity.

Prolonged sun exposure

UV rays damages the fibres in your skin called elastin. When these fibres break down, your skin begins to stretch and lose its ability to go back into place before tsretching.

Processed foods

Most processed foods have mnay chemicals and additives that produce free radicals which can cause inflammation and premature aging. Foods high in salt can make your skin hold more water resulting to your skin looing puffy.
